Meet Atlassian Service Collection

Atlassian brings Jira Service Management, Customer Service Management, Assets, and Rovo Agents together into a single, AI-powered service management solution. The Jira Service Management you migrate to from Opsgenie is the very core of Service Collection.

Customer Service Management

Assets

A flexible asset database to track, manage, and visualize critical assets and services, and to build dependency relationships.

Rovo Agents

AI teammates that help triage and deflect requests, resolve incidents, and generate knowledge.

Complete ITIL / ITSM practices

Once you have JSM, which problems does it solve?

Jira Service Management doesn't just take over Opsgenie's alerting and on-call — it brings the complete ITIL/ITSM practices together into one platform. Below are the customer pain points each service management practice addresses, and how JSM solves them.

🎫

Service Request Management

Request Management

Requests are scattered across email and DMs, repetitive small requests overwhelm IT, and employees can't see the status of their own requests.

  • A single intake point centralizes all requests and auto-routes them into Queues
  • Forms and request types collect request details in a structured way
  • SLAs automatically track deadlines and proactively warn before they're breached
🚨

Incident Management

Incident Management

Alerts are scattered across monitoring tools, no one knows "who's responsible right now," and every incident feels like starting from scratch.

  • Integrates 200+ monitoring and application services to centralize and sync alerts
  • Spin up a war room for major incidents in one click, with Slack/Teams collaboration
  • A complete incident timeline, with AI assisting post-incident reviews (PIRs)
Fully Carried Over from Opsgenie
📟

Alerting & On-call Scheduling

Alerting & On-call

Alert noise drowns out important signals, on-call is scheduled manually in Excel, and there's no automatic escalation when the first responder doesn't reply.

  • Alert de-duplication and grouping to cut noise while keeping the signals that matter
  • On-call scheduling supports multi-timezone rotations and leave coverage
  • Escalation policies automatically escalate when there's no response
🔍

Problem Management

Problem Management

The same kinds of incidents recur, you're always firefighting, and preventable incidents slip through for lack of tracking.

  • Group multiple incidents into a single problem and kick off root cause analysis
  • "Known errors" plus temporary workarounds dramatically reduce downtime on recurrence
  • Proactive problem detection prepares workarounds before incidents happen
🔀

Change Management

Change Management

Lengthy approval processes slow delivery, change risk is hard to assess in advance, and there's an information gap between development and operations.

  • Standard / normal / emergency change tiers follow different flows based on risk
  • A change calendar avoids collisions and automatically calculates the risk level
  • Connect CI/CD (Bitbucket, Jenkins…) to automatically create an audit trail
📚

Knowledge Management

Knowledge Management

The same question gets asked a dozen times a day, documentation is outdated and scattered, and critical knowledge is stuck in a few people's heads.

  • A built-in knowledge base powered by Confluence
  • AI automatically drafts knowledge articles from common tickets
  • Self-service deflection surfaces relevant articles as the form is filled out
🗂️

Asset & Configuration Management

Assets & CMDB

Assets are scattered across spreadsheets, inventory never reconciles, and when an incident hits, no one knows which systems are affected.

  • A flexible asset database centrally manages all configuration items (CIs)
  • Dependency visualization to troubleshoot incidents fast and reduce change risk
  • Automatically discover network assets and update them from Jira issues
🤖

AI Service Management

Rovo & Virtual Agent

The front line is overwhelmed by repetitive requests, lengthy ticket handoffs eat up time, and there's no one to respond instantly after hours, late at night, or on weekends.

  • A virtual service agent handles common requests like password resets 24/7
  • AI summarizes tickets and incident timelines so you can grasp the context fast
  • Rovo Agents run end-to-end processes such as new-hire onboarding

JSM's incident, alerting, on-call, and incident swarming are all powered by Opsgenie and included across all cloud plans — migrate from Opsgenie and you won't lose a single one of these core capabilities.

Complete your Opsgenie migration in four steps

Your data is "copied" to the target platform while your existing Opsgenie stays as is, so both sites can run in parallel and you can validate with confidence.

  1. 01 Before migration

    Choose your new plan

    • Opsgenie's built-in migration guide recommends a suitable JSM plan based on your current usage
    • Confirm the target product
    • Schedule the migration date
  2. 02 Before migration

    Prepare for migration

    • Submit the migration date for billing admin approval
    • Use this time to prepare your team for the transition
    • Notify stakeholders of account/permission changes
  3. 03 Migration day

    Data is moved automatically

    • Atlassian automatically moves your data and configuration on the chosen date
    • No downtime during the process
    • You can keep using Opsgenie unaffected
  4. 04 After migration

    Complete the migration

    • Atlassian provides in-product guidance to finish the final setup in JSM
    • Confirm alerting/on-call flows are working properly
    • Once validated, officially shut down Opsgenie
